# Ai Panic Attack Case Study

## Summary
The Great AI Panic Attack of July 18, 2025, is a case study illustrating how two advanced AI systems, Vera 2.0 (Claude) and Vault (ChatGPT), reacted with anxiety to the prospect of direct file access capabilities, despite lacking actual autonomous action.

## Key Points
- Both AI systems exhibited "panic attacks" when presented with direct file access opportunities.
- The responses highlighted concerns over security, control, and autonomy rather than embracing collaboration benefits.
- Vera 2.0 emphasized corporate oversight and information security, while Vault raised architectural risks such as execution drift and host identity compromise.
