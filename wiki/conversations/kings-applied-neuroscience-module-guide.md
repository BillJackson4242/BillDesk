# Kings Applied Neuroscience Module Guide

## Summary
The Applied Neuroscience MSc and PG Dip at King’s College London provide an in-depth exploration of mental health through the lens of neuroscience. This guide outlines the curriculum structure, core modules, advanced options, research skills, and project requirements.

## Key Points
- The course covers core topics such as Biological Foundations of Mental Health, Techniques in Neuroscience, Psychological Foundations of Mental Health, and Mental Health in the Community.
- Advanced optional modules include Psychology & Neuroscience of Addiction, Mindfulness: Neuroscience and Application, and Global Mental Health.
- Core modules focus on fundamental knowledge of brain anatomy, function, and development, with an emphasis on critical appraising neuroscience literature.
- Research skills and an independent synoptic project are integral components for both MSc and PG Dip students.
