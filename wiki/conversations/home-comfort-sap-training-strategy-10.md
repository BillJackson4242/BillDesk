# Home Comfort Sap Training Strategy 10

## Summary
This document outlines a training strategy for Home Comfort to implement SAP Fit-to-Standard across 30 locations with diverse workforces. The goal is to equip employees with necessary skills and minimize disruption during the transition.

## Key Points
- **Role-based Training**: Tailored learning paths for Order Entry Staff, Warehouse Associates, Supervisors, Accounts Payable, and All Employees.
- **Performance Mindset**: Emphasizes practical application over system details in training content.
- **Blended Delivery Channels**: Combines instructor-led virtual sessions, job aids, video snippets, simulations, and train-the-trainer programs to cater to different learning styles.
