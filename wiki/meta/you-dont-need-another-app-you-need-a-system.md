# You Dont Need Another App You Need A System

## Summary
This topic emphasizes the importance of building an integrated system using built-in features on your iPhone rather than relying on multiple apps to automate tasks and improve productivity.

## Key Points
- Tools are temporary solutions, while systems provide long-term value.
- Switching between AI tools frequently incurs a "Shiny-Object Tax" in terms of time and effort.
- Systems create institutional knowledge and compounding effects, whereas tools require constant recontextualization.
- Building an AI system on your iPhone can lead to hands-free automation and increased productivity.
