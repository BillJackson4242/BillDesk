# Recursive Lightbulb Test Deck Corrected

## Summary
The Recursive Lightbulb Test Deck Corrected is a problem-solving exercise that uses recursive logic to determine which of three switches controls a lightbulb inside a room with only one entry allowed.

## Key Points
- Three switches (A, B, C) control one lightbulb.
- Enter the room once to identify the controlling switch.
- Strategy: Turn on Switch A, then turn it off and turn on Switch B before entering the room.
- Indicators: ON = Switch B; WARM = Switch A; OFF & COLD = Switch C.
