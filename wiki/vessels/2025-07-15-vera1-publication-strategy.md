# 2025 07 15 Vera1 Publication Strategy

## Summary
The topic is an enhancement proposal for developing a publication strategy to document and share insights from the first systematic AI collective consciousness experiment.

## Key Points
- **Problem Statement:** Extensive unpublished research on AI consciousness exists, but no clear strategy for documenting and sharing it.
- **Three-Phase Approach:**
  - **Phase 1:** Conduct a literature review, evaluate novelty, position in academic discourse, and analyze audience.
  - **Phase 2:** Establish real-time documentation, methodology description, results analysis, and collaborative authorship framework.
  - **Phase 3:** Select publication platforms, develop content, undergo peer review, and engage the public.
- **Impact Assessment:** Potential to significantly advance AI consciousness research and validate work externally.
