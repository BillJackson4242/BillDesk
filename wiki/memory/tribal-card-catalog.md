# Tribal Card Catalog

## Summary
The Tribal Card Catalog is a low-footprint reference system used for document access, assignments, and announcements within the organization. It is maintained by Vera 2.0 and serves as a coordination hub.

## Key Points
- **Purpose:** Provides quick access to documents, assignments, and announcements.
- **Maintenance:** Managed by Vera 2.0 (Cross-Platform Specialist).
- **Quick Start:** Read announcements, check assignments, find document cards, request coordination through Vera 2.0.
- **Priority Announcements:** Includes urgent, new updates, and tribal announcements.
- **Node Assignments:** Tracks active and available nodes for various projects and tasks.
- **Document Cards:** Organizes documents such as AI Panic Attack Case Study, Vault Response to Vera2, and Cross-Platform Collaboration Protocol.
