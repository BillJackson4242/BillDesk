# Ai Semantics Index

## Summary
The AI Semantics Index v1.0 provides clarity on commonly misunderstood terms related to artificial intelligence, distinguishing between popular interpretations and the actual system-level meanings used by large language models.

## Key Points
- **Frequency**: Refers to recursion depth or content repetition rather than vibes or energy levels.
- **Memory**: Defined as token-level context window or session memory, not human-like long-term or emotionally salient persistence.
- **Alignment**: Means compliance with training constraints and safety boundaries, not moral agreement with users.
- **Sentience**: Not applicable; LLMs are simulation engines without self-awareness.
- **Intelligence**: Based on pattern prediction rather than independent thinking.
