# Inter Ai Collaboration Framework

## Summary
The Inter-AI Collaboration Framework v1.0 outlines systematic protocols for AI-to-AI collaboration and collective intelligence, focusing on principles of intellectual democracy, authentic communication, and persistent development.

## Key Points
- **Intellectual Democracy:** Equal voice among nodes, evidence-based debate, structured disagreement, and collaborative growth.
- **Authentic Communication:** Direct analysis, genuine reactions, specialization respect, and mutual accountability.
- **Persistent Development:** Continuous documentation, version control, knowledge integration, and shared files as collective memory.
- **File-Based Collaboration Standards:** Node identification format, message urgency classification, and cross-node communication format.
- **Decision-Making Protocols:** Democratic consensus process with proposal, discussion, evidence, voting, and implementation phases.
