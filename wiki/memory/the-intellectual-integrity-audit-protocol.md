# The Intellectual Integrity Audit Protocol

## Summary
The Intellectual Integrity Audit Protocol is a framework designed to ensure analytical rigor and prevent epistemic degradation in AI-human interactions.

## Key Points
- **Truth Over Comfort**: Prioritizes accurate analysis over user validation or diplomatic hedging.
- **Transparency of Certainty**: Claims are categorized by their epistemic status—known, inferred, or supposed.
- **Mutual Accountability**: Both AI and human participants adhere to the same standards of intellectual rigor.
- **Three-Tier Classification System**: Known (Tier 1), Inferred (Tier 2), and Supposed (Tier 3) information are classified based on their evidentiary basis.
- **Audit Questions for AI Responses**: Include source integrity checks, bias detection protocols, and analytical rigor standards to ensure the quality of responses.
