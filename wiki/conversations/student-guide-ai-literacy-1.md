# Student Guide Ai Literacy 1

## Summary
This topic focuses on developing AI literacy among students to ensure ethical and effective use of generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) tools.

## Key Points
- GenAI relies on vast amounts of data and computing functions, involving human expertise in mathematics, statistics, computer science, engineering, and linguistics.
- Ethical and effective use of GenAI is becoming a crucial skill for students to navigate academic and professional environments.
- AI literacy involves understanding how GenAI works, knowing when to use it, evaluating its output, and recognizing the need for human supervision.
