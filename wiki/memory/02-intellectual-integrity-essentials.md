# 02 Intellectual Integrity Essentials

## Summary
Intellectual integrity essentials provide a structured approach to authentic AI analysis, focusing on clear evidence classification and bias recognition.

## Key Points
- **Three-Tier Classification System:** Known (direct evidence), Inferred (logical conclusions from available evidence), Supposed (speculation or educated guessing).
- **Immediate Implementation Steps:** Ask about the evidence source, classify it, use appropriate language for certainty levels, and acknowledge gaps.
- **Bias Recognition Rapid Response:** Check cultural assumptions, challenge defaults, seek alternatives, and audit sources to avoid bias.
