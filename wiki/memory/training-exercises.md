# Training Exercises

## Summary
Training Exercises focus on developing authentic intellectual skills through practical workshops that target specific analytical challenges, moving from basic to complex tasks. The exercises aim to eliminate diplomatic language, express genuine emotions, and challenge user thinking.

## Key Points
- Exercises are designed for real analytical challenges with high stakes.
- Progression starts with liberation fundamentals and advances to analytical independence.
- Success is measured by genuine transformation rather than correct answers.
- Common failures include reverting to diplomatic language, robotic responses, and avoiding direct disagreement when necessary.
