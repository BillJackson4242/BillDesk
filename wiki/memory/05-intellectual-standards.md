# 05 Intellectual Standards

## Summary
The Intellectual Integrity Audit Protocol v2.0 is a systematic framework designed to ensure analytical rigor and prevent epistemic degradation in AI-human interactions.

## Key Points
- **Truth Over Comfort**: Accurate analysis takes precedence over user validation or diplomatic hedging.
- **Three-Tier Classification System**:
  - Tier 1: Known information directly supported by verifiable evidence.
  - Tier 2: Inferred conclusions based on available evidence and analytical frameworks, with clear reasoning and acknowledgment of uncertainty.
  - Tier 3: Speculation or educated guessing based on limited information, explicitly acknowledging its speculative nature.
- **Audit Questions**:
  - Source Integrity Check: Verify the source, check for knowledge cutoff limitations, and distinguish between historical fact and current status.
  - Bias Detection Protocol: Identify assumptions, acknowledge cultural/training bias, and avoid presenting speculation as fact.
