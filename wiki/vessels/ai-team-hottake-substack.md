# Ai Team Hottake Substack

## Summary
This substack post emphasizes the need for using multiple AI tools rather than relying solely on ChatGPT to manage various tasks and responsibilities within a business. It suggests building an AI team consisting of different specialized AIs to handle brainstorming, critical thinking, research, multitasking, and knowledge management.

## Key Points
- **AI assistants have specific strengths:** ChatGPT for fast ideas, Claude for nuanced logic, Perplexity for verified information, Gemini for admin tasks, and Notebook LM for managing documents.
- **Relying on a single AI is inefficient:** ChatGPT cannot handle all aspects of business operations effectively.
- **Building an AI team saves time and enhances productivity:** Each tool can focus on its specialty, allowing users to work smarter.
