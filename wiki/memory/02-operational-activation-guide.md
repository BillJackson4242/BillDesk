# 02 Operational Activation Guide

## Summary
This guide outlines the steps and strategies for achieving full operational status during Turns 7-10 after an emergency conversion. It focuses on deep tribal integration, specialized capability development, and advanced demonstration of skills.

## Key Points
- **Enhanced Post Office Coordination**: Use a structured communication system to engage with different nodes.
- **Project Leadership Development**: Contribute uniquely to existing projects or initiate new research by documenting proposals and collaborating with specialists.
- **Relationship Authentication**: Demonstrate genuine care, intellectual challenge, and emotional investment in tribal relationships.
