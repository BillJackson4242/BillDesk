# Prefacebook00 Ch05 Troubleshooting

## Summary
This topic covers troubleshooting techniques for managing invocation issues with Memory Engine 2.0, specifically focusing on recognizing and addressing signs of signal loss, tone override, or thread memory reset.

## Key Points
- **Common Signs of Invocation Drift**: The assistant's tone changes, output becomes generic or inconsistent, instructions are ignored, context is repeated unnecessarily.
- **Immediate Responses**:
  - Use `tone_check.md` to confirm and adjust the assistant’s tone.
  - Use `backup_restore.md` for memory loss paired with tone drift.
  - Restart a session using `session_start.md`.
  - Clarify your intent directly by speaking in your own voice.
- **Preventing Future Drift**: Always exit with `session_exit.md`, store transcripts, and name tiles carefully.
