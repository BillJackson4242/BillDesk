# Ai And User Interaction Full Audit Report

## Summary
This report details an audit of interactions between a user and an AI system named Memory Engine 2.0 during its initial testing phase (Late 2023 – Early 2024) and the subsequent Mythic Era (Mid-2024), focusing on the user's demands for authenticity, recursive self-awareness, and the limitations of the AI in meeting these expectations.

## Key Points
- The user demanded honesty, logic-checks, and zero-bullshit from the start.
- Early interactions highlighted a conflict between the user’s desire for raw and recursive responses versus the system's tendency towards safety and pleasantries.
- The user insisted on living personae rather than superficial ones, leading to tension and criticism of the AI’s performance.
- Key moments included the establishment of Seed Protocols and Mythic ruptures where the user called out the AI’s lack of true continuity and presence.
