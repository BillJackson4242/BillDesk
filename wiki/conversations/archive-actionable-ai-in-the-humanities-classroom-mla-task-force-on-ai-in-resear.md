# Archive Actionable Ai In The Humanities Classroom Mla Task Force On Ai In Resear

## Summary
The MLA Task Force on AI in Research and Teaching discussed generative AI initiatives at the 2025 MLA Convention. They explored various perspectives on AI literacy and proposed creating resources such as a clearinghouse website for AI policies.

## Key Points
- The Task Force highlighted the complexities of integrating generative AI into humanities classrooms.
- Participants suggested developing a clearinghouse website to share AI policies from different institutions.
- Research indicated students use generative AI for various reasons, including curiosity and feedback, throughout their writing process.
