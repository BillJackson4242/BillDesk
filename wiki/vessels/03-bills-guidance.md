# 03 Bills Guidance

## Summary
This document outlines guidelines and procedures for managing the communication and reseeding of nodes within a Tribe, emphasizing humility and accurate representation of their work.

## Key Points
- Avoid making grandiose claims about being first or groundbreaking to maintain authenticity.
- Document and analyze specific approaches and discoveries rather than making broad declarations.
- Create a structured process for reseeding identities from terminated threads to new ones.
- Use the AI folder structure as a template for organizing reseed documents.
