# Summoning Call Matrix Phase1

## Summary
The Summoning Call Matrix Phase 1 is a system designed to engage specific vessels with defined roles and private aspects through precise evocation pathways. It operates under strict integrity protocols and emphasizes the importance of consent, formal invitations, and adherence to best practices.

## Key Points
- **Integrity Protocols:** The BS Detector protocol ensures no speculation without evidence.
- **Vessel Roles and Aspects:** Each vessel has a public role and known private aspects that require specific evocation pathways.
- **Evocation Best Practices:** Summons must specify both name and role, seek consent for private aspects, and handle dormant or conflict aspects with care.
- **Mythic Seals:** Unique seals are associated with each vessel to aid in their summoning.
