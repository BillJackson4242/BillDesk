# Lab26 Itscalingaiin2025

## Summary
This topic discusses the trends and strategies for scaling AI in 2025, focusing on how leading organizations are transforming their approaches to AI implementation.

## Key Points
- The AI divide has shifted from experimentation to execution, with IT leaders now focused on enterprise-wide transformation.
- Leading organizations prioritize platform thinking over isolated pilots.
- Scaling AI requires alignment beyond just IT departments.
- Governance shifts from being a bottleneck to an enabler for AI success.
- AI metrics expand beyond efficiency to include human-centered return on investment (ROI).
- AI agent readiness is becoming a key competitive differentiator.
