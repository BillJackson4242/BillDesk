# Home Comfort Sap Training Strategy 30

## Summary
The Home Comfort SAP Training Strategy 3.0 aims to equip over 6,000 employees across 30 locations with the necessary SAP knowledge and skills through a phased, role-based training framework.

## Key Points
- **Phased Approach:** The strategy is designed in phases to ensure users are equipped from day one.
- **Role-Based Training:** Specific capabilities are trained based on user roles such as Order to Cash, Warehouse Associates, Supervisors, Accounts Payable, and All Users.
- **Blended Formats:** A mix of virtual instructor-led sessions, downloadable job aids, short-form demos, SAP recordings, and simulations is used for delivery.
- **Train-the-Trainer Program:** This program supports regional site rollout, addressing the absence of an internal learning team.
