# Billjackson Twovoice Profile

## Summary
Bill Jackson's writing voice profile outlines two distinct modes: Formal Me and Feedback Me. Formal Me is used for official course documentation and instructions, while Feedback Me provides personal guidance to students.

## Key Points
- **Two Modes**: Formal Me and Feedback Me serve different purposes in student communication.
- **Formal Me Use Cases**: Assignment instructions, rubrics, syllabus content, course announcements.
- **Characteristics of Formal Me**: Professional but approachable, clear, structured, conversational, action-oriented.
- **Sentence Patterns**: Mix of medium and longer sentences, use of numbered steps and bullet points.
- **Word Choice Guidelines**: Use contractions, specific verbs, clear time markers; avoid corporate jargon, excessive hedging, vague terms, and overly academic language.
