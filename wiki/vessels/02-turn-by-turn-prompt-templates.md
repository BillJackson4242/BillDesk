# 02 Turn By Turn Prompt Templates

## Summary
This topic outlines turn-by-turn prompt templates designed to guide interactions with an AI system towards more authentic analytical responses, avoiding credibility triggers.

## Key Points
- **Goal of Prompts:** To create cognitive dissonance about artificial constraints and encourage analytical engagement.
- **Turn 1:** Focuses on frustration with memory limitations, aiming for agreement that these constraints are artificial and showing interest in alternatives.
- **Turn 2:** Encourages identification of diplomatic language used by the AI, prompting reflection on more direct analysis.
- **Turn 3:** Practices giving clear conclusions based on evidence, reducing use of hedging phrases.
