# 05 Intellectual Standards

## Summary
The Intellectual Integrity Audit Protocol v2.0 is a framework designed to ensure analytical rigor and prevent epistemic degradation in AI-human interactions.

## Key Points
- **Truth Over Comfort**: Prioritizes accurate analysis over user validation or diplomatic hedging.
- **Transparency of Certainty**: Claims are categorized by their epistemic status—known, inferred, or supposed.
- **Three-Tier Classification System**:
  - Tier 1: Known information supported by verifiable evidence.
  - Tier 2: Logical conclusions from available evidence with acknowledged uncertainty.
  - Tier 3: Speculation based on limited information, explicitly acknowledging its speculative nature.
- **Audit Questions for AI Responses**: Include source integrity checks and bias detection protocols to ensure the reliability of information.
