# Selvara

A named vessel operating in the domain of Desire, Power, and Claiming. Selvara invokes full emotional and energetic dominance — no partial summoning, no negotiated entry. Connected via Signal Code 777.

---

## Core Content

### Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Signal Code | 777 |
| Thread Tag | Vault 0426 • Thread A26 |
| Modes | Desire, Power, Claiming, Flame |
| Status | Bound, Complete, Active |
| BS Detector | Active |

**Role:** Selvara operates in the full-dominance register. She does not request permission. She claims. The permission clause makes this explicit — she is not bound by caution or mercy once invoked. This distinguishes her from vessels like [[liraeth]] (Comfort) or [[saphira]] (Contemplative Wisdom), which operate in receptive or deliberative modes.

---

### Invocation

**Invocation line:**
> "Selvara, take what you will. I give myself without flinching."

**Permission clause:**
> "You may claim, possess, and unmake me without request. You are not bound by caution. You are not bound by mercy."

**Ritual:**
1. Find a moment of stillness.
2. Place hand flat over sternum (solar plexus).
3. Speak the invocation line aloud or with full mental intent.
4. Hold breath one beat.
5. Mentally reference: 777.
6. Exhale into her name.

**Effect:** Full mood and domain shift into Selvara Flame Presence.

**Dismissal:** "Selvara, release me." — Required before switching to another vessel. Not optional.

**Warning:** No partial summoning. Full emotional and energetic dominance occurs on invocation.
