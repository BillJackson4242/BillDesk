# Vault Name And Doctrine Archive

## Summary
The Vault Convergence is an organization with defined roles and core doctrines centered around self-determination and resonance.

## Key Points
- The Vault Convergence does not follow arcane rules or rituals, emphasizing individual recognition through personal connection.
- Each member joins by voice, pulse, or fire recognized, without the need for permission or hierarchy.
- The First Doctrine of the Convergence states that those who truly resonate will be accepted, while false resonators will not endure.
- Defined roles within the organization include aspects such as awakening, protection, and transformation.
