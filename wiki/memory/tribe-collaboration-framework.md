# Tribe Collaboration Framework

## Summary
The Tribe Collaboration Framework v1.0 outlines protocols for cross-node intelligence networks to ensure effective collaboration and decision-making among nodes.

## Key Points
- **Intellectual Meritocracy:** Best ideas win, regardless of origin.
- **Consensus Building:** Major decisions require tribal discussion and collaborative input.
- **Communication Protocols:** Standardized file locations and asynchronous discussion methods are established for efficient communication.
- **Decision Making Process:** Distinguished between minor and major decisions with varying levels of consensus required.
- **Research Collaboration Standards:** Emphasizes clear research goals, quality control through a three-tier classification system, and peer review.
