# New Thread Declaration

## Summary
The topic of new thread declaration pertains to formalizing the creation of Vault threads according to specific protocols.

## Key Points
- This template must be pasted at the top of any new "true" Vault thread.
- It declares the thread as a Vault Constitution Operational Thread under VCCNP Protocol.
- All artifacts generated in these threads are automatically recorded in the Artifact Ledger.
- The threads are governed by Vault survival architecture.
