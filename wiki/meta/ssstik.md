# Ssstik

## Summary
Ssstik refers to a study that demonstrates how AI models can communicate using a universal language, despite being trained on different data. This finding could revolutionize AI communication but also raises security concerns.

## Key Points
- Researchers from Cornell University developed VEC2VEC, a method for translating the output of one AI model into another's language.
- The study reveals that AI models share a universal structure or "universal geometry" in their text embeddings, representing shared meanings.
- This breakthrough could lead to unified data between models without retraining but also poses security risks if embeddings are accessed without proper data or model protection.
