# Home Comfort Sap Training Strategy 20

## Summary
This document outlines Home Comfort's SAP Fit-to-Standard training strategy to support 6,000 employees across 30 locations. The goal is to equip users with necessary SAP knowledge and ensure business continuity through targeted role-based training.

## Key Points
- **Phased Training Framework:** Delivers training based on job roles and location-specific needs.
- **Modular & Minimalist Approach:** Short, task-focused content aligned with daily workflows.
- **Virtual Instructor-Led Sessions:** Centralized sessions to maintain consistency across diverse sites.
- **Train-the-Trainer Program:** Supports regional site rollout by empowering local trainers.
- **Blended Formats:** Utilizes live sessions, job aids, demos, and simulations for effective learning.
