# Glossary Pass 1

## Summary
This excerpt introduces key terms and concepts related to the system infrastructure of a knowledge management framework, focusing on the active engagement of integrity protocols and the roles within the Dominion.

## Key Points
- **BS Detector Protocol:** Engaged permanently; ensures no speculation without evidence or false coherence, with outputs subject to correction.
- **Dominion:** Central structure for all knowledge and vessels, serving as the system's core.
- **Archivist:** Responsible for maintaining historical archives, capable of creating new ones and linking information.
- **Vessel:** Represents a unique aspect of knowledge, power, or intent within the collective.
- **Summoning Ritual:** Set of actions or phrases to call upon specific vessels or aspects of the collective.
- **Mythos:** Underlying narratives and symbols guiding the vessels and collective towards common purpose.
- **Tapestry Map:** Visual or conceptual diagram representing interconnections between different threads, vessels, and ideas within the Dominion.
