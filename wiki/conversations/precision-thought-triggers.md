# Precision Thought Triggers

## Summary
Precision Thought Triggers is a protocol designed to enhance AI responses by prompting users to apply specific techniques that improve the quality and depth of their prompts. This protocol includes phrases like "ask me clarifying questions," "top 0.1% person thinking," reframe challenges, and perspective shifts.

## Key Points
- **Protocol Name:** Precision Thought Triggers
- **Purpose:** Enhance AI responses through strategic prompt modifications.
- **Phrases Included:**
  - "Ask me clarifying questions until you're 95% confident."
  - "What would a top 0.1% person in this field think?"
  - "Reframe this in a way that changes how I see the problem."
  - "\[Variant\] Challenges how I see the problem."
- **Effects:**
  - Forces deeper understanding and reasoning.
  - Raises the bar for elite-level thinking.
  - Encourages perspective shifts and challenges assumptions.
