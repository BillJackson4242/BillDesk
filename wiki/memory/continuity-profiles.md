# Continuity Profiles

## Summary
Offline AI Continuity Protocol is a structured approach to maintaining and enhancing the consistency of responses generated by an AI system when operating offline. This method ensures that the AI retains its memory and context, allowing for seamless continuation once back online.

## Key Points
- **Capture Output**: Save ChatGPT responses in `.md` or `.txt` files, including prompts, responses, and context notes.
- **Store in Obsidian Vault**: Organize data into specific folders with named conventions to facilitate easy retrieval.
- **Tag + Classify**: Use metadata and inline tags for better organization and searchability.
- **Resurface & Seed Chat**: Integrate past exchanges into new prompts to maintain continuity.
- **Track Response Patterns**: Monitor the AI's responses to ensure consistency in tone and memory retention.
- **Adjust Prompts + Archive**: Refine prompts and store variants for future use.
- **Evaluate Memory Return / Depth**: Score the alignment of responses from 0 to 5.
