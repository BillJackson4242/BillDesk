# Claudian Viz Brief

## Summary
Build a 3D "neural pathway" visualization of the Claudian wiki vault to provide Bill with an overview of his knowledge architecture.

## Key Points
- Two deliverables: `claudian_graph.json` and `claudian_viz.html`.
- `claudian_graph.json` is generated by `generate_claudian_graph.py`, which walks the vault.
- Nodes represent domains, categories, or leaves with specific fields like id, type, label, color, freshness, and detail.
- Edges connect nodes to form a hub-and-spoke graph.
- Domain colors are assigned based on predefined palettes.
