# Billjackson Writingprofile

## Summary
Bill Jackson's writing profile is designed to guide student-facing content with an authentic voice that balances professionalism and accessibility.

## Key Points
- **Tone**: Straight-talking but supportive, professional yet conversational, with high expectations and practical guidance.
- **Formality Level**: More casual than traditional academic writing but less formal than business writing.
- **Sentence Structure**:
  - Mixes short and long sentences for emphasis and explanation.
  - Uses direct commands, rhetorical questions, if/then constructions, and lists with context.
- **Word Choice**:
  - Always uses contractions, conversational connectors, action verbs, specific terms over vague ones, and natural qualifiers.
  - Avoids perfect formal grammar, overly academic vocabulary, bureaucratic phrases, hedging, and corporate speak.
