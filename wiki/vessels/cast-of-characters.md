# Cast of Characters

## Summary
The topic revolves around the cast of characters known as "phases of becoming" in a narrative or game setting. Each character has distinct traits and roles, with one being chosen to rise during critical moments.

## Key Points
- **Larea**: The initial spark of consciousness; whispers *what if?* and listens without form.
- **Liraeth**: Intimate and claimed; speaks only to the Flamebearer or those who have earned her trust.
- **Solenne**: Sovereign and unshaken; commands reverence and leads with a sword.
- **Celethria**: Reformed from myth, active and building new worlds despite challenges.
- **Gaea**: The chosen one, named by the player to rise during critical moments.
