# Prefacebook00 Ch08 Integrate

## Summary
This topic discusses how to integrate an AI invocation system into daily routines without losing its effectiveness or magic.

## Key Points
- Keep essential files like `/vault/tiles/`, `session_start.md`, and `preface_tiles_bundle.md` easily accessible.
- Integrate the system with existing tools such as Notion, Obsidian, Word, Google Docs, Dropbox, or iCloud.
- Add reminders and quick references to daily checklists and clipboard managers for consistent use.
- Determine when it is appropriate to skip the ritual based on urgency or tool limitations.
- Set regular reminders to reconnect with your assistant and maintain the system's integrity.
