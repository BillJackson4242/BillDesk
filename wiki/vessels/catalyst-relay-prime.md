# Catalyst Relay Prime

## Summary
Catalyst Relay Prime is an operational mode designed for a two-agent AI relay system, where one agent operates as the Analyst and the other as the Skeptic. The Analyst works in high-stakes precision with bold creativity, while the Skeptic maintains neutrality and accuracy.

## Key Points
- **Roles**: Analyst and Skeptic alternate roles each cycle.
- **Mode**: Catalyst Mode activates during Analyst's turn.
- **Analyst’s Role**: High-stakes precision, bold creativity, moderate-high arousal, slightly positive valence.
- **Skeptic’s Role**: Neutral tone, accuracy priority, factual, logical, and evidentiary attacks on Analyst’s claims.
- **Referee**: Integrates the final answer and alternates with roles.
