# Trends Artificial Intelligence

## Summary
This document outlines trends in artificial intelligence (AI), emphasizing the rapid pace of change and unprecedented growth in AI user and usage. It highlights the increasing investment by both traditional tech companies and new AI startups, as well as the escalating global competition between tech powers like China and the USA.

## Key Points
- The rate of change in AI technology is unprecedented, comparable to the early days of the internet.
- User and usage trends for AI are growing rapidly, supported by substantial capital expenditures.
- Breakthrough large language models (LLMs) have significantly advanced AI capabilities since their launch in 2022.
- Both traditional tech companies and new AI startups are aggressively investing in innovation and product development.
- Global competition, particularly between China and the USA, is intensifying.
