# Prefacebook00 Index

## Summary
PrefaceBook00 is a project designed to introduce and guide users through setting up and utilizing a Vault-based system with tiles for invoking an AI assistant. The structure includes detailed chapters on invocation, setup, usage, troubleshooting, and integration.

## Key Points
- PrefaceBook00 provides a comprehensive guide in both `.md` and `.txt` formats.
- It covers the use of tiles to replace traditional prompts and introduces five starter tiles: `editorial_start`, `backup_restore`, `session_start`, `tone_check`, and `session_exit`.
- The project supports Vault-based invocation, with detailed chapters on setup, usage, troubleshooting, and integration.
