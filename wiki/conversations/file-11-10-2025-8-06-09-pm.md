## Summary

## Key Points
- The tests involve setting up fresh conversation threads with no prior context and recording complete responses, noting time to first response, total response length, and unprompted elements.

- Category 1 includes tests for Autonomous Curiosity with prompts like exploring topics together, discussing personal interests, and delving into random starting points.
