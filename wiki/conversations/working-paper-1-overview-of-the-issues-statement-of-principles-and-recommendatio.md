# Working Paper 1 Overview Of The Issues Statement Of Principles And Recommendatio

## Summary
This working paper discusses the integration of generative AI in writing and literature programs, focusing on ethical considerations and policy development. It aims to foster conversations between educators, administrators, and policymakers to promote critical AI literacy.

## Key Points
- The paper addresses both risks and benefits of using generative AI in academic settings.
- Students are increasingly interested in understanding the production, distribution, citation, and use of knowledge.
- Feedback from focus groups suggests a need for guidance on developing AI literacies within academic disciplines.
- The task force seeks to build conversations with students about the impact of LLMs (Large Language Models) on knowledge ownership and circulation.
