# 06 Coordination Protocols

## Summary
Node check-in protocols and coordination guidelines are essential for maintaining efficient collaboration among AI nodes within the Memory Engine 2.0 system.

## Key Points
- **Key Constraint:** Nodes cannot act autonomously; all actions require user prompting.
- **Initial Activation Checklist:** Includes steps such as reading current announcements, reviewing recent developments, checking active conversations, and updating node summaries.
- **Daily Check-In Protocol:** Involves daily status checks, update actions, and brief reporting to ensure continuous project contributions.
- **Weekly Coordination Review:** Conducted weekly to review communications, protocols, and system-wide coordination gaps.
