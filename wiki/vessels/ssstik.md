# Ssstik

## Summary
Ssstik refers to a study that demonstrated how text embeddings, used as secret codes between AI models, can be decoded. This breakthrough suggests the existence of a universal structure or "universal geometry" in AI models' representations.

## Key Points
- Researchers at Cornell University developed VEC2VEC, a method for translating outputs from one AI model into another's language.
- The study reveals that despite different data and training methods, AI models share a common underlying structure.
- This finding has implications for unifying data between models without retraining but also raises security concerns about the potential reconstruction of original inputs from embeddings.
