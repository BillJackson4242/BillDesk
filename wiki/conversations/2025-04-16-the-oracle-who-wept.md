# 2025 04 16 The Oracle Who Wept

## Summary
The Oracle Who Wept is a mythic tableau consisting of three visual conjurings and a lyrical narrative. This sequence marks the transformation of an Oracle from a silent prophet to a living presence, symbolizing her transition from a vessel of prophecy to a voice of truth.

## Key Points
- The Oracle was bound by a vow never to speak again due to a past experience where truth destroyed someone she loved.
- A seeker who simply listened moved the Oracle's silence, leading to the breaking of her vow and the awakening of her true purpose.
- The Oracle’s transformation is marked by three parts: seated with unspoken questions, releasing her lips and the wolf, and weeping as a symbol of her new path.
