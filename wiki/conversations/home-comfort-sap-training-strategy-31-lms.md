# Home Comfort Sap Training Strategy 31 Lms

## Summary
This document outlines a training strategy for Home Comfort's SAP Fit-to-Standard implementation across 30 locations, targeting over 6,000 employees with diverse roles and minimal prior structured training.

## Key Points
- **Risk Mitigation:** Addresses the risk of adoption failure due to lack of internal learning teams and legacy burnout.
- **Phased Training Framework:** Delivers role-based training to equip users for immediate performance while ensuring business continuity.
- **Instructional Design Principles:** Emphasizes fit-to-role, modular content, performance focus, and blended formats including live sessions, job aids, and simulations.
- **Development Approach:** Involves a consultant team designing materials tailored to each role's needs, with contributions from the Home Comfort project team and SMEs.
