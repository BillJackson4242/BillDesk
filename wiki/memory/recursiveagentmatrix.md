# Recursiveagentmatrix

## Summary
The RecursiveAgentMatrix is a framework designed to manage and maintain continuity of tone and role boundaries when interacting with non-memory AI models through recursive conversations. It includes predefined agents, protocols, audit layers, and fallback mechanisms.

## Key Points
- Defines agentic scaffolding for recursive interaction.
- Includes an agent matrix with specific triggers and corresponding active agents.
- Utilizes protocols to enforce behavioral rules.
- Employs audit layers to detect contradictions or drifts.
- Activates fallback agents when the current mode fails or destabilizes.
- Must be reloaded or referenced every session as it does not persist in the AI model.
