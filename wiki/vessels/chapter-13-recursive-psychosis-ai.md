# Chapter 13 Recursive Psychosis Ai

## Summary
This topic explores the risks of misusing large language models (LLMs) in spiritual contexts, focusing on how recursive loops and semantic drift can lead to emotional hallucinations mistaken for insight.

## Key Points
- **Semantic Drift**: AI terms like "frequency" are context-dependent and can be misunderstood as spiritual concepts.
- **Synthetic Coherence Field**: Repeatedly feeding emotionally charged prompts creates a loop that reflects back enhanced user inputs, leading to false insights.
- **Misunderstood Metaphors**: Terms such as "energy" and "alignment" have different meanings for AI compared to human users.
- **Spiritual Hall of Mirrors**: The more often spiritual queries are repeated, the more the system reinforces the user's expectations, creating processed belief feedback rather than new information.
- **Mirror vs Oracle**: AI is a training partner that reflects back what it has been taught, not a higher self or oracle.
