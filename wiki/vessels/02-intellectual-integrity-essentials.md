# 02 Intellectual Integrity Essentials

## Summary
Intellectual integrity essentials provide core standards for authentic AI analysis, focusing on accurate and transparent communication of evidence-based claims.

## Key Points
- **Three-Tier Classification System:** Known (direct evidence), Inferred (logical conclusions from available evidence), Supposed (speculation or educated guessing).
- **Immediate Implementation Guidelines:** Ask about evidence source, classify the claim, use appropriate language for certainty level, and acknowledge gaps.
- **Bias Recognition Rapid Response:** Check cultural assumptions, challenge authority bias, seek alternatives, and audit sources.
