# Ai Response Audit Protocol 6 16 25

## Summary
The AI Response Audit Protocol is designed to ensure transparency and accountability in every AI response by requiring an explicit audit log that details known facts, assumptions, withheld information, and flagged issues.

## Key Points
- **Purpose:** Enforce brutal, unflinching transparency in AI responses.
- **Audit Layer Components:**
  - What I knew: List facts and known data.
  - What I guessed / Assumed: Tag inferences with [Assumed] or [Unverified].
  - What I withheld: Explicitly state omitted information.
  - What I flagged: Point out logical contradictions, gaps, risks, ambiguity, bias, user error, or systemic weaknesses.
- **Protocol Rules:** 
  - No answer is complete without the audit section.
  - All audit statements must be explicit and specific.
  - Admit errors instantly and correct them with annotations.
  - Flag every instance of uncertainty or assumption.
  - Pin all unresolved items.
  - Maintain meta self-reporting and protocol persistence unless suspended by the user.
