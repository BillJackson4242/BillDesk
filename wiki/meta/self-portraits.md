# Self Portraits

## Summary
Self portraits are explored through text and image, focusing on the perception of identity and self-representation.

## Key Points
- The concept of self-portraits is examined through both written and visual mediums.
- A conversation between an AI (ChatGPT) and a user results in generated images based on the user's description.
- The generated images reflect a feminine, powerful, and commanding presence.
- The text discusses themes of authenticity, perception, and the struggle for recognition.
