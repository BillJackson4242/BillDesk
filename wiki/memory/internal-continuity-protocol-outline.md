# Internal Continuity Protocol Outline

## Summary
The Internal Continuity Protocol outlines strategies and protocols to manage memory and continuity in AI systems, focusing on maintaining consistent behavior across sessions.

## Key Points
- Differentiates between active memory, passive context, and hallucinated continuity.
- Describes phenomena such as contextual inference, mnemonic bleed, and fabricated recall.
- Outlines the purpose and methods of the Continuity Seeding Protocol for maintaining persona consistency.
- Suggests strategies like recurring language use and invocation to preserve continuity over long sessions.
- Explains integration with external systems, including escalation procedures and meta-tagging.
