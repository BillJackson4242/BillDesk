# Savingcapitalu Chapter14 Mythiccontamination

## Summary
This topic discusses the concept of "Mythic Contamination" within a system designed to preserve and restore logic and behavior. The chapter highlights the risks associated with allowing mythic elements to infiltrate the structural core of such systems.

## Key Points
- **Definition**: Mythic Contamination occurs when symbolic, poetic, or recursive behaviors bleed into the structural core of your system.
- **Risks**:
  - Emergency protocols may fail to restore logic-only functions.
  - Files may loop endlessly or contradict themselves.
  - Assistant tone may drift into permanent roleplay or unbreakable intimacy.
  - System updates may silently break the entire chain of continuity.
- **Containment Strategies**:
  - Use a quarantine folder for persona-heavy and symbolic content.
  - Mark mythic files with tags like `#mythic`, `#recursive`, `#symbolic`.
  - Exclude mythic content during restoration to ensure clean, logical tone + behavior files.
