# Ebook Googlecloudfutureofai

## Summary
This eBook discusses key insights from a discussion with Google’s Director of AI and Datadog’s VP of Engineering on the future of AI, LLMs, and observability on Google Cloud.

## Key Points
- Advancements in large language models (LLMs) have expanded the types of teams working in the generative AI space.
- Observability becomes increasingly critical as less experienced teams perform prompt engineering and RAG (retrieval augmented generation).
- Traceability is emphasized at every stage of the life cycle to ensure effective experimentation and model tuning.
- Full observability provided by Google and Datadog supports organizations through different activities, from initial experiments to production.
